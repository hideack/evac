
### rss

Subscription in RSS.

#### Parameters

| Name       | parameter |
|------------|----------------------------|
| url        | Feed URL. |
| uniqueness | Uniqueness |
| format     | Passed subscription format. |

      "url": "http://feeds.feedburner.com/hideack?format=xml",
